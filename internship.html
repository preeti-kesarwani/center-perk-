About

         We founded Coffee Bond with one goal in mind: providing a high-

             quality coffee to our neighbors. We draw inspiration from

                the coffee culture of Melbourne, with its rainbow

       coffees,deconstructed lattes, communal tables, and unique cafes, 

            which take on the spirit of the neighborhood that they are 

               ensconced within. Founder, Narendra, wanted to recreate a
               
       similar vibe in Delhi when he returned after spending 10 years

            in Melbourne, and that’s how Coffee Bond came about.

               “I saw how small businesses, run by driven and passionate

        individuals, made Melbourne into a most liveable city.

            These small businesses were interconnected — a person

             making cheese would supply to a friend’s bakery, who would supply

        the baked goodies to another friend’s café,” says Narendra,

            who saw this interconnected web of associations being

        created to increase customer experience as well, and inspired him

              to start a specialty coffee shop.
